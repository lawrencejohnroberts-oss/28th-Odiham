Welcome to 28th Odiham (Dogmersfield and Crondall)

Skills for Life • Adventure • Friendship

Join us for exciting adventures, make lifelong friends, and develop skills that will last a lifetime. We welcome young people aged 4-14 in our vibrant scouting community.

Our Scout Sections

Adventure awaits at every age!

Squirrels

Ages 4-6

Our youngest adventurers! Squirrels enjoy games, crafts, and outdoor fun while learning basic skills and making new friends.

Tuesdays 4:45-5:45 PM
Games Crafts Stories Nature

Beavers

Ages 6-8

We have two Beaver sections! Both have fun with friends while learning new skills through games, crafts, and exciting activities both indoors and outdoors.

Eagle Beavers: Tuesdays 6:00-7:15 PM
Kestrel Beavers: Thursdays 6:00-7:15 PM
Badge Work Outdoor Games Community Creativity

Cubs

Ages 8-10½

We have two Cub packs! Both take on bigger adventures, work towards activity badges, and develop independence through camping and outdoor activities.

Adventurers Cub Pack: Mondays 6:30-8:00 PM
Pioneers Cub Pack: Wednesdays 6:30-8:00 PM
Camping Hiking Skills Teamwork

Apaches Scout Troop

Ages 10½-14

The Apaches Scout Troop takes on leadership roles, plan their own adventures, and work towards their Chief Scout's Award through challenging activities.

Tuesdays 7:30-9:15 PM
Leadership Expeditions Adventure Service

Explorers (Ages 14-18)

For our older young people, we're connected with other groups in the Odiham District to provide Explorer opportunities. Contact us for more information about Explorer units in our area.

What We Do

Adventure, skills, and fun for everyone!

Camping & Outdoor Adventures

From weekend camps to summer adventures, we explore the great outdoors and learn essential outdoor skills.

Arts & Crafts

Creative activities that develop fine motor skills, imagination, and artistic expression for all age groups.

Games & Sports

Fun indoor and outdoor games that build teamwork, fitness, and friendship among our young people.

Adventure Activities

Hiking, climbing, water sports, and other exciting activities that challenge and inspire our scouts.

Badge Work & Skills

Structured learning through activity badges, developing practical skills and knowledge across many areas.

Community Service

Giving back to our local community through service projects and helping those in need.
